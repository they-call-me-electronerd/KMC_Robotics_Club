FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience to join?

No! We welcome members of all skill levels. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced developer, there's a place for you in our club.

Is there a membership fee?

There's a minimal annual membership fee that covers workshop materials, equipment access, and event participation. The exact amount will be communicated during the onboarding process.

How much time commitment is required?

We recommend dedicating at least 3-4 hours per week for club activities, including meetings, workshops, and project work. However, this is flexible based on your schedule.

Can non-KMC students join?

Currently, membership is limited to Kathmandu Model College students. However, external participants can attend our open workshops and public events.
