Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience?

No! We welcome members of all skill levels. Whether you're a beginner or have advanced experience, there's a place for you in our club.

What are the membership fees?

Membership is free for all KMC students. We may collect a nominal donation for club activities and project materials.

How often do we meet?

We hold regular meetings and workshops throughout the year. The schedule is flexible to accommodate most members' availability.

Can non-KMC students join?

Currently, membership is primarily for KMC students. However, we welcome community participants in our public events and workshops.

What equipment do I need?

The club provides most robotics equipment and tools. You just need to bring enthusiasm and commitment to learning!
