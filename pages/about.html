Our Mission

Building Tomorrow's Innovators

KMC Robotics Club is a student-led technical community at Kathmandu Model College dedicated to fostering innovation, creativity, and technical excellence. We bring together passionate students who share a common goal: to explore the frontiers of robotics, artificial intelligence, and emerging technologies.

Our mission is to provide a collaborative platform where students can learn, experiment, and create solutions that address real-world challenges. Through hands-on projects, workshops, and competitions, we empower the next generation of engineers and innovators.

Our Vision

To become a leading hub for technological innovation in Nepal, nurturing world-class engineers and researchers who will shape the future of robotics and AI.

Our Values

We believe in collaboration over competition, continuous learning, ethical innovation, and creating inclusive spaces where every voice is heard and valued.

Our Impact

We're building a community of problem-solvers who tackle challenges from sustainable energy to healthcare automation, making technology accessible to all.

What We Do

Our Focus Areas

Robotics & Automation

Design and build autonomous robots, from line followers to advanced robotic arms, exploring mechanical design and control systems.

AI & Machine Learning

Explore neural networks, computer vision, and natural language processing to create intelligent systems that learn and adapt.

IoT & Embedded Systems

Connect devices and sensors to build smart systems for home automation, environmental monitoring, and industrial applications.

Workshops & Training

Regular sessions on programming, electronics, 3D printing, and emerging technologies to build skills from beginner to advanced levels.

Our Journey

Milestones & Achievements

2023
Club Foundation

KMC Robotics Club was established with a vision to create a thriving tech community at Kathmandu Model College, starting with 15 founding members.

2024
First Robotics Competition

Participated in our first inter-college robotics competition, securing 2nd place with our autonomous line-following robot project.

2024
AI Workshop Series

Launched a comprehensive workshop series covering Python, machine learning, and computer vision, training over 100 students.

2025
Innovation Lab Inauguration

Opened our dedicated robotics lab equipped with 3D printers, electronics workstations, and development kits for hands-on learning.

Present
Growing Community

Today, we're a vibrant community of 50+ members working on cutting-edge projects and preparing for national-level competitions.
