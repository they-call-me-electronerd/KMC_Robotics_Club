About Our Club

Empowering students to become the next generation of innovators through hands-on robotics and AI education

Our Story

Founded in 2024, the Kathmandu Model College Robotics Club (KMC RC) began with a simple vision: to create a space where students passionate about technology could come together to learn, build, and innovate.

What started as a small group of enthusiasts has now grown into a thriving community of over 50 active members, working on cutting-edge projects that span robotics, artificial intelligence, IoT, and embedded systems. Our club has become a launchpad for future engineers, providing hands-on experience that goes far beyond textbooks.

We believe that innovation happens when curious minds collaborate. Through workshops, competitions, and real-world projects, our members gain practical skills while solving problems that matter. From building autonomous robots to developing AI-powered solutions, we're pushing the boundaries of what's possible.

Our Mission

To provide students with practical, hands-on experience in robotics and artificial intelligence, fostering innovation, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills that prepare them for careers in cutting-edge technology fields.

Our Vision

To become the leading student robotics organization, recognized nationally for producing innovative projects, winning competitions, and developing the next generation of technology leaders who will shape the future of AI and robotics.
